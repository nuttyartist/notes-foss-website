FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How my notes are stored?

All your notes are simple plain text strings under the hood. All these plain text strings are stored in a SQLite database locally on your computer. We are considering plans to remove the reliance on a custom database and to store all notes as simple .txt files inside a folder.

Have more questions?

Write to us if you have more questions. If your question is a frequent one, we will add it here. You can reach us at contact@notes-foss.com.
